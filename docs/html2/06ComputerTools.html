Chapter 6: Computer Tools

SoX Sound Exchange Library - Bagwell

Chris Bagwell is the current maintainer of the SoX Sound Exchange library, described at the Source Forge web site.

Click sox thought.raw thought.wav to use sox to convert thought.raw to thought.wav (only click on this link after 1st running the sox conversion).
Click sox thought.raw thought.wav reverb to convert thought.raw to thought.wav with added reverb (only click on this link after 1st running the sox conversion).
