Brainwave Enterrtainment through Binaural Sound - Thompson

These examples demonstrate brainwave entrainment by stimulating each ear with similar frequency sine waves the brain will 'beat' at a frequency corresponding to the difference of the two sinewaves. Consequently headphones must be used with this demo. The program takes two arguments, the 'beat' frequency and the carrier frequency. The following demos use a carrier of 400Hz:

  • Delta 3Hz (entrain 3 400)
  • Theta 6Hz (entrain 6 400)
  • Alpha 10Hz (entrain 10 400)
  • Beta 20Hz (entrain 20 400)

Use caliper to control the delta and carrier frequencies: Brain tune (../../../bin/caliper.exe "entrain %f %f" -i 10 400 -m 0 1)
