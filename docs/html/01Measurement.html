Sound Pressure Levels: "Mine Goes to 11!" - Greenebaum

This demo illustrates an amplitude ramp. Specifically, this demo plays a series of sounds at decreasing amplitudes. If the amplitude differences are smaller than the perceptual just noticeable difference, then, the series appears to be a single continuous sound of decreasing volume instead of the series of sounds they actually are. Parameters were selected to minimize discontinuities by changing amplitude only at zero crossings.

 

Run amplitudeRamp 0.9 to play this demo with small steps. Run amplitudeRamp 0.2 for much more rapid and noticeable steps.

 

Run caliper "amplitudeRamp %f" -i 0.9 -f 1 -m 1 to play this demo instrumented with the "caliper" adjustment tool, starting at 0.9.  Press + or – to increase or decrease the attenuation rate by 0.1.
