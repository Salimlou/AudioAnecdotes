Sound Pressure Levels: "Mine Goes to 11!" - Greenebaum

This demo illustrates an amplitude ramp. Specifically, this demo plays a series of sounds at lower and lower amplitudes. The amplitude differences are smaller than the just noticeable difference, and hence, appear to be a single continuous sound of decreasing volume instead of the series of sounds they actually are. Parameters were selected to minimize discontinuities by changing amplitude only at zero crossings.

Run amplitudeRamp 0.9 to play this demo. Run amplitudeRamp 0.2 for a more rapid ramp and noticably discrete sounds.
